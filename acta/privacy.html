Acta — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Overview

Acta is designed to work without an account. We collect the minimum data needed to run the service, and we never sell your data.

Data we collect

  • Anonymous device identifier — a random UUID generated on first launch. It is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.
  • Your goals and records — everything you track (goals, daily records, notes) is stored locally on your device. There is no account, and your entries are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Goal text during plan creation — when you create a goal, the goal description and your questionnaire answers are sent to our AI provider to generate your plan. This text is processed transiently to produce the plan and is not used to train AI models.
  • Anonymous usage events — basic events such as app opens, completed records, and goal creation (never the text of your goals or notes), used to understand how the app is used and to improve it.

What we don't do

  • No account or sign-up is required; we do not collect your name, email, or contacts.
  • No advertising SDKs, no cross-app tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive anonymized purchase state (e.g. whether a subscription is active) to unlock features, but we never see your payment details.

Data retention & deletion

Usage data is tied to a random device identifier, not to your identity. Deleting the app removes the identifier from your device; you may request deletion of associated server records via the contact below.
