MBTI Focus Meditate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

MBTI Focus Meditate is designed to work without an account. We collect the minimum data needed to run the service, and we never sell your data.

Data we collect

  • Anonymous device identifier — a random UUID generated on first launch. It is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.
  • Your selected MBTI type — used to personalize music recommendations.
  • Playback feedback — play, like, dislike, and skip events with the session type, sent to our server to improve track curation for each personality type.

What we don't do

  • No account or sign-up is required; we do not collect your name, email, or contacts.
  • No advertising SDKs, no cross-app tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive anonymized purchase state (e.g. whether a subscription is active) to unlock features, but we never see your payment details.

Data retention & deletion

Usage data is tied to a random device identifier, not to your identity. Deleting the app removes the identifier from your device; you may request deletion of associated server records via the contact below.
