Invime — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

Invime is designed to work without an account. We collect the minimum data needed to run the service, and we never sell your data.

Data we collect

  • Anonymous device identifier — a random UUID generated on first launch. It is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.
  • Your skin data — your skin profile, product experiences, reactions, routines, and wishlist are stored locally on your device. There is no account, and this data is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Ingredient lists during analysis — when you request a fit analysis or an ingredient explanation, the product's ingredient list together with anonymized signals from your profile (skin type, concerns, and reaction summaries — never your name or contact details) is sent to our AI provider. This data is processed transiently to produce the result and is not used to train AI models.
  • Anonymous usage events — basic events such as app opens, product views, and routine completions (never the contents of your skin profile or reaction notes), used to understand how the app is used and to improve it.
  • Product catalog — the app downloads a shared, public catalog of products and ingredients from our server. This is a read-only download; no personal data is attached to it.

What we don't do

  • No account or sign-up is required; we do not collect your name, email, or contacts.
  • No advertising SDKs, no cross-app tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive anonymized purchase state (e.g. whether a subscription is active) to unlock features, but we never see your payment details.

Data retention & deletion

Usage data is tied to a random device identifier, not to your identity. Deleting the app removes the identifier from your device; you may request deletion of associated server records via the contact below.
